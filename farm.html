About Village to City

We're revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain by creating a direct connection between rural farmers and urban consumers.

Our platform eliminates unnecessary intermediaries, ensuring farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work while city dwellers get access to fresh, organic produce at reasonable prices.

Through our innovative logistics network, we bridge the geographical gap between villages and cities, creating a sustainable ecosystem that benefits everyone involved.

How It Works

Our streamlined process connects farmers directly with consumers

1. Farmers Register

Rural farmers register on our platform, providing details about their produce, farming practices, and available quantities.

2. Products Listed

Farmers list their products with transparent pricing. Consumers can browse through a wide variety of fresh, organic produce.

3. Orders Placed

City consumers place orders directly from farmers. Our system aggregates orders from the same region to optimize logistics.

4. Logistics Network

Our optimized logistics network picks up produce from farms and delivers it to distribution centers in the city.

5. Home Delivery

Fresh produce is delivered directly to consumers' doorsteps, often within 24-48 hours of harvesting.

6. Fair Payment

Payments are processed securely, with farmers receiving their fair share directly, without multiple intermediaries taking cuts.
